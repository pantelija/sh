Saffiano leather watch straps

Leather straps with a textured, cross-hatch finish that comes in many colors. Saffiano straps are long-lasting and resistant to water and scratches.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

When used in watch straps, Saffiano leather results in a strong, durable band that is available in various colors and able to resist water and scratches.

But let us explain what Saffiano leather actually is. Saffiano is a method of embossing leather, rather than a specific type of leather.

A specialized stamping machine creates the cross-hatch finish. Color comes from coating the leather with resin. This procedure covers the base material. This is why some companies use plastic or faux leather as a base material, so shop carefully.

  • More resistant to water compared to other types of leather
  • Scratch resistant and durable
  • Saffiano bands come in many colors
