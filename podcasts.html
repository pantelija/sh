Watch Podcasts

In this blog post, we'll showcase some of the best watch-themed podcasts out there so you can start listening, stay in the know about the latest news, releases, and events, and have lots of fun.

Nenad Pantelic â€¢ April 24, 2023



Podcasts have become one of the most popular ways to consume information and entertainment. And for watch enthusiasts, there are many really good shows available

From interviews with industry leaders to discussions about the latest releases and trends, these podcasts offer a tons of knowledge and entertainment for all enthusiasts.

So sit back and enjoy the best watch-related content available.

Got a suggestion? Reach out to us and we'll be happy to give it a listen, and feature the podcast on this list.


The Grey Nato

Jason Heaton & James Stacey

"A loose discussion of travel, adventure, diving, driving, gear, and most certainly watches". Let's be real: TGN is among the best watch podcast out there.

Listen on Spotify

Standard h Podcast

Wesley Smith

This is not nesesarly watch only podcast, but Wesley is super knowledgable on the topic. Wesley really does his research and asks great questions. Top content.

Listen on Spotify

The Viva Bastardo Show

Phil Toledano

Not for people who: love alligator straps, ignore poor kerning, buy waitlisted watches, worry about dings and scratches, hate boxy cars, enjoy contemperary Rolex... Braaap!

Listen on Spotify

Blamo!

Jeremy Kirkland

Blamo! digs into the world of fashion and the influential figures who shape it, including designers, creative directors, actors, athletes and watch people, of course.

Listen on Spotify

Hodinkee radio

Hodinkee team & James Stacey

The show covers important updates from the watch industry. James is joined by various Hodinkee team members and guests for interesting and fun discussions.

Listen on Spotify

OT: the podcast

Andy Green & Felix Scholz

Show revolves around the world of watches, timekeeping, and how to make the most of it. The podcast features interviews with guests, and covers the latest releases, news, and topics in the industry.

Listen on Spotify

worn and wound podcast

Worn and Wound team

Every week W&W engages in relaxed discussions with friends and professionals from the watch industry. They cover the most recent news, advancements, events, and watch releases.

Listen on Spotify

Fratello On Air

Fratello team & Michael, Balazs, Nacho

Laid-back conversations between Fratello team members and occasional guests, covering a range of topics: new releases, news, meetups, and collecting in general.

Listen on Spotify

Collectability podcast

John Reardon

On his show John Reardon engages in discussions with experts who are well-versed and knowledgeable in the world of watches, particularly Patek Philippe.

Listen on Spotify

about effing time

George, Adrian, & Andrew

Although they come from different backgrounds, the trio shares a common interest and passion for watches, which has brought them together. No stone is left unturned. Highly recommended.

Listen on Spotify

two broke watch snobs

Michael Penate & Kaz Mirza

A podcast centered around watches where two friends engage in in weekly, candid conversations with a touch of profanity, covering various topics related to their hobby and passion.

Listen on Spotify

beyond the dial

Allen Farmelo

Allen Farmelo hosts a podcast that digs into the world of watches, exploring watch movements, industry news, collecting, and insights about the brands. Allen's passion is visible in every episode, he is a true enthusiast.

Listen on Spotify

A blog to watch weekly

A Blog To Watch Team

A weekly podcast featuring news and reviews from around the watch world as seen through the eyes of Ariel, Michael, and David form ABTW.

Listen on Spotify

The Time 4A Pint Podcast

Chris Mann

Chris runs a podcast where he chats with enthusiasts, collectors, and professionals in the watch industry, discussing their love for watches, recent developments in the field, newly released products, and other watch-related topics.

Listen on Spotify

watch and listen podcast

Matt Farah & Cameron Weiss

Watch And Listen is a video podcast about the world of horology, watch collecting, and watchmaking. Hosts Matt Farah of The Smoking Tire and Cameron Weiss of the Weiss Watch Company take a deep dive into the world of watches.
