The Best Watch Podcasts

In this blog post, we'll showcase some of the best watch-themed podcasts out there so you can start listening, stay in the know about the latest news, releases, and events, and have lots of fun.

Nenad Pantelic • August 4, 2024 (the latest update)



Podcasts have become one of the most popular ways to consume information and entertainment. And for watch enthusiasts, there are many really good shows available

From interviews with industry leaders to discussions about the latest releases and trends, these podcasts offer a tons of knowledge and entertainment for all enthusiasts.

So sit back and enjoy the best watch-related content available.

Got a suggestion? Reach out to us and we'll be happy to give it a listen, and feature the podcast on this list.


The Grey Nato

Jason Heaton & James Stacey

"A loose discussion of travel, adventure, diving, driving, gear, and most certainly watches". Let's be real: TGN is among the best watch podcast out there.

Listen on Spotify

Fratello On Air, Fratello Talks

Fratello team & Michael, Balazs, Nacho

Laid-back conversations between Fratello team members and occasional guests, covering a range of topics: new releases, news, meetups, and collecting in general.

Listen on Spotify

The Deep Track

Blake Buettner

Blake Buettner is on a mission to find the coolest stories in watches. From dive bars to mountaintops, he is chatting with geeks, adventurers, and people from watches and other fun industries.

Listen on Spotify

worn and wound podcast

Worn and Wound team

Every week W&W engages in relaxed discussions with friends and professionals from the watch industry. They cover the most recent news, advancements, events, and watch releases.

Listen on Spotify

Hodinkee radio

Hodinkee team & Tony Traina

The show covers important updates from the watch industry. Tony is joined by various Hodinkee team members and guests for interesting and fun discussions.

Listen on Spotify

OT: the podcast

Andy Green & Felix Scholz

Show revolves around the world of watches, timekeeping, and how to make the most of it. The podcast features interviews with guests, and covers the latest releases, news, and topics in the industry.

Listen on Spotify

Standard h Podcast

Wesley Smith

This is not nesesarly watch only podcast, but Wesley is super knowledgable on the topic. Wesley really does his research and asks great questions. Top content.

Listen on Spotify

Openwork: Inside the Watch Industry

Asher Rapkin and Gabe Reilly

Openwork offers an insider's look at the watch industry: in-depth explorations of specific watch models, analysis of industry trends, a deep dive into the nuances of watch collecting, and explanations of the evolving watch world landscape.

Listen on Spotify

Form & Function

James Crichton, Philip Pantelides, and Ken Lam

Form & Function dives deep into the world of watches, everyday carry, tech, fitness, and lifestyle products. Enjoyable listen. Oh yeah, titanium everything. And Veblenist.

Listen on Spotify

Spirit of Time Podcast

Matt and Gregg

Two SoCal watch geeks who also happen to love good food and drink. They chat about watches, whiskey, wine, and whatever else catches their fancy.

Listen on Spotify

Hairspring

Erik Gustafson and Max Braun

A casual, weekly deep dive into the world of horology. From the complexity of watch collecting to fun, geeky topic. Expect plenty of laughs, industry behind-the-screens, and genuine passion for the craft.

Listen on Spotify

Scottish Watches

Rikki Bruce & Nicholas Bowman Scargill

The Scottish Watches Podcast is all about the latest watch stuff. They chat about new watches, cool events, and whatever else is going on in the watch world.

Listen on Spotify

The Vintage Watch Podcast

Charlie Dunne and Eric Wind

An in-depth exploration of vintage watch collecting, popular and less-known references, market shifts, new releases, and updates from the watch industry.

Listen on Spotify

This Watch Life

Lydia Winters and Vu Bui

A daily watch podcast covering watches, collecting, the community, new releases, and the overall watch industry. Yes, daily! You read that right.

Listen on Spotify

Collectors Gene Radio

Cameron Ross Steiner

Collectors Gene Radio goes deep into the intricacies of collecting. Colectors and experts share their stories and collecting principles. There are many episodes that are dedicated to the world of watch collecting.

Listen on Spotify

two broke watch snobs

Michael Penate & Kaz Mirza

A podcast centered around watches where two friends engage in in weekly, candid conversations with a touch of profanity, covering various topics related to their hobby and passion.

Listen on Spotify

Collectability podcast

John Reardon

On his show John Reardon engages in discussions with experts who are well-versed and knowledgeable in the world of watches, particularly Patek Philippe.

Listen on Spotify

40 and 20: the WatchClicker Podcast

Andrew and Everett

"40 and 20" focuses is on affordable watches, but they also enjoy exploring topics such as food, drinks, everyday essentials, popular culture, and life in general.

Listen on Spotify

Making Time Podcast

Darren Caplan

Darren from Zulu Alpha Straps sits down with some of the names in watches to chat about their lives and businesses. It is like hanging out with your watch-obsessed friend and getting the inside scoop on the industry. No suits, just real talk about watches.

Listen on Spotify

beyond the dial

Allen Farmelo

Allen Farmelo hosts a podcast that digs into the world of watches, exploring watch movements, industry news, collecting, and insights about the brands. Allen's passion is visible in every episode, he is a true enthusiast.

Listen on Spotify

A blog to watch weekly

A Blog To Watch Team

A weekly podcast featuring news and reviews from around the watch world as seen through the eyes of Ariel, Michael, and David form ABTW.

Listen on Spotify

Superlative with Ariel Adams

Ariel Adams

Industry media veteran Ariel Adams talks about the nitty-gritty details of the watch world, sharing behind-the-scenes insights, lesser-known industry facts, and other interesting topics. A thoughtful and entertaining listen.

Listen on Spotify

Wrist Check Pod

Perri, Ben, and Rashawn

Wrist Check is a weekly watch podcast hosted by three friends passionate about watches. Each episode dives into new watch releases, industry news, and engaging interviews with watch experts and enthusiasts.

Listen on Spotify

Blamo!

Jeremy Kirkland

Blamo! digs into the world of fashion and the influential figures who shape it, including designers, creative directors, actors, athletes and watch people, of course.

Listen on Spotify

The Time 4A Pint Podcast

Chris Mann

Chris runs a podcast where he chats with enthusiasts, collectors, and professionals in the watch industry, discussing their love for watches, recent developments in the field, newly released products, and other watch-related topics.

Listen on Spotify

The Viva Bastardo Show

Phil Toledano

Not for people who: love alligator straps, ignore poor kerning, buy waitlisted watches, worry about dings and scratches, hate boxy cars, enjoy contemperary Rolex... Braaap!

Listen on Spotify

about effing time

George, Adrian, & Andrew

Although they come from different backgrounds, the trio shares a common interest and passion for watches, which has brought them together. No stone is left unturned. Highly recommended.

Listen on Spotify

watch and listen podcast

Matt Farah & Cameron Weiss

Watch And Listen is a video podcast about the world of horology, watch collecting, and watchmaking. Hosts Matt Farah of The Smoking Tire and Cameron Weiss of the Weiss Watch Company take a deep dive into the world of watches.

Watch on YouTube
