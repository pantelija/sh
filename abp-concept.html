About ABP Concept

Atelier du Bracelet Parisien is a multi-generational team led by the founder, Jean-Claude Perrin. He gained recognition in the watch strap industry by establishing two companies, Camille Fournet and Creations Jean-Claude Perrin, dedicated to supplying watch straps to Swiss watch companies.

In 1997, he founded a unique workshop near Place Vendôme in Paris. This workshop offered a one-of-a-kind concept where watch enthusiasts and collectors could personally create their own custom watch straps. The workshop had an extensive selection of fifty different leathers and tannings, hundreds of colors for leathers and stitching, and the ability to choose the exact part of the leather to be used for crafting their straps.

The workshop quickly gained popularity, leading Jean-Claude to bring his wife, Regine, his son, Yann, his brothers-in-law, Dominique and Pierre, and a team of skilled craftsmen to join him in expanding the venture.
