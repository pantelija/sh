What types of straps does Dangerous9straps sell?

Leather Custom

About Dangerous9straps

Dangerous9straps creates custom, hand-crafted luxury watch straps for various watch brands. They use traditional techniques to produce high-quality leather and exotic skin straps. Until recently their straps were previously only sold to watch collectors as a bespoke products.

Recently, Dangerous9straps launched a Manufacture Collection, which offers straps made by their Italian manufacture partners to the same high standards as their fully bespoke straps. The collection uses the same materials, construction methods and lining leathers as the bespoke straps, with only slight trade-offs in terms of variety and upgrades.

The Manufacture Collection straps are indistinguishable from the fully bespoke straps. They are available for a more competitive price with a faster turnaround time of 10 days.
