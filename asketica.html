What types of straps does Asketica sell?

Asketica exclusively offers two-piece leather bands, bund straps and single pass leather straps.

Leather

Width: Their bands are made in all widths from 16mm to 24mm. You can order either tapered or straight straps.

Tapering is usually 2mm, but for the 22mm and 24mm straps you can choose more pronounced tapering (for example 20mm/16mm or 22mm/18mm).

Material: Asketica uses a variety of leather types, including Buttero, Pueblo, Horween cromexcel, and calfskin.

About Asketica Watch Bands & Bund Straps

Asketica was founded in 2015 inspired by a love of leather and a desire to create elegant and functional products. The company believes in surrounding oneself with beautiful simple things, which influences one's way of thinking and living.

They aim to provide customers with timeless and handcrafted watch straps that are affordable and can be customized to fit their own sizes. The straps are made from high-quality leather sourced from the best tanneries in the world.

Asketica prioritizes style, comfort, and fast delivery, making their straps a unique finishing touch to any outfit.
