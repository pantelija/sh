What types of straps does Delugs sell?

Here is the list of strap types they offer:

Leather Elastic Canvas Custom

Width: Their most popular models are made in widths of 18mm, 20mm, and 22mm. If you need a strap for watches with 17mm or 21mm lug widths, then you can find several options in their two-piece collections.

Material: Initially, Delugs had a limited selection of high-quality leather straps, crafted using materials like Shell Cordovan, Chromexcel, Buttero, and Epsom.

As they expanded, they introduced new types of leather like Babele, Pueblo, Waxy, and Russian Hatch Grain, providing customers with a range of options they may not have been previously exposed to.

Recently, they started offering elastic straps made from stretchy nylon.

About Delugs

The founders of Delugs have a background in leather crafting and watch collecting. They noticed a gap in the market for high-quality, well-crafted leather watch straps that were also affordable.

They set out to create a collection of straps that were made using the best materials and construction methods, but produced in large enough quantities to keep costs down. They launched Delugs in 2018 and have since expanded their product line and built a strong following among watch collectors.
