Alligator leather watch straps

Alligator is an excellent choice when you want to dress up your watch and showcase elegance. These exotic straps are known for their unique pattern and pliability.

Why we love them?

These straps are highly desirable for their stylish appearance, but once you put them on, you realize it is not all about the looks. They feel great on the wrist.

  • Alligator is a very durable leather
  • Straps are supple, silky, and very flexible
  • Short break-in period
  • Elegant and sophisticated look

Average price of an alligator leather strap

Genuine alligator leather is a premium, exotic material, and watch straps made from it are understandably more expensive than straps made from other types of leather.

Prices for alligator straps can start from $150, with an average range between $190 and $250.
