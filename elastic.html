Elastic watch straps

Elastic watch straps have an awesome ability to stretch and move with the wearer, allowing the watch to remain securely on the wrist during activities.

We we love them?

They are extremely comfortable straps. Also, elastic bands are quite durable, ensuring that they can withstand the rigors of military or professional use. And yeah, they are good enough for your desk diving.

  • They are easily adjustable
  • Provide snug and comfortable fit
  • They come in a range of colors and styles
  • No break-in period required

Average price of an elastic strap

Majority of elastic straps are usually on the affordable side. Prices fit into the range between $20 and $30.

Indeed, more expensive options are available. These straps are made from premium material that will last longer and prices for these straps start from $70.

Common types of elastic straps

There are several types of elastic watch straps that are commonly used. These include:

  • Elastic NATO-style watch straps
  • Two-piece elastic straps
  • Hook & Loop elastic straps
  • Paratrooper elastic straps
