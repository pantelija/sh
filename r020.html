Review of the Pink FKM Rubber Strap by Brodinkee & StrapHabit

The strap is made from FKM rubber. Two pieces are 120mm and 75mm long, each with a ridge along its length. The tapering is 2mm, and the stainless steel buckle is signed. Additionally, the strap has integrated quick-release spring bars.

The Full Review

Cancer is a complicated disease. It doesn't only affect the person diagnosed with it but also impacts the lives of those closest to them, including family and friends.

It leaves a lasting mark. Coping with cancer is a battle that requires massive strength and support.

For that reason, any initiative aimed at supporting those affected deserves recognition. The collaboration between StrapHabit and Brodinkee is a wonderful partnership that, for a couple of years, has been raising awareness about cancer and financially contributing to a greater cause.

StrapHabit offers this strap in widths of 20mm and 22mm. Apart from selecting different widths, you can select either the stainless steel or black PVD buckle.

Design and Materials

We covered the FKM straps in detail before. The same features and properties remain here for this Ridge FKM strap.

Rubber is pliable and soft, finishing is matte, and the material does not attract dust.

The strap feels substantial and sturdy. There are integrated quick-release spring bars. The buckle is heavy-duty, made from stainless steel, and signed. There is a small Easter egg in the etched StrapHabit name. You'll figure it out.

A few words about the color. It really pops, but it is not too vibrant and punchy. Actually, it works pretty well, especially if you pair the strap with black, grey, or white dial watches.

Comfort and Durability

The underside of the strap has an embossed channel. This prevents the strap from sticking to your skin while you're wearing it. The channel creates just enough spacing so your wrist can breathe.

Overall, the strap goes nicely around the wrist, fits well, and doesn't move. Even with bigger and heavier watches, I've had no issues. The strap can handle such watches.

Yep, this FKM strap is really comfy. What's even better is that it feels comfy right from the start, you don't need to wear it in.

One thing to mention: The Ridge FKM strap is thick. It's 5mm thick on one side and 3mm on the other. You'll definitely feel it on your wrist, there's no way you'll forget you're wearing a watch.

Initial Usage Strap looks nicely, feels premium and is well made. Wearing it on is quite enjoyable and comfortable experience. No break-in period was needed, which is always a big plus.

Two months of use Looks and feels as if it is new. Zero issues or problems. Quick release spring bars are still sturdy, keepers still hold excess part firmly. I think this strap will last for a long time.

Compatibility and Pairing Recommendation

Of course I will take the easy way out and write that this strap fits all kinds of watches. Dress, sports, pilots, chronographs, field watches or vintage...

Put the StrapHabit x Brodinkee collab strap on and enjoy its fun pink color for as long as you want. Spread the word about the cause and invite other watch enthusiasts to buy one.
