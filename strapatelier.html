About Strapatelier

Kai, the founder & owner, started a watch strap company in Singapore after developing a love for fine timepieces and customizing his own watch straps. He aims to create high-quality, unique straps at affordable prices and uses materials sourced from around the world, with a focus on environmentally friendly vegetable-tanned leather.

The team is working hard to achieve exceptional quality and durability in their handcrafted straps, and they hope to build a community of watch enthusiasts.
