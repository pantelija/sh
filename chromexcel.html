Chromexcel leather watch straps

Soft and durable watch straps that have a rich color vibrancy and that quickly develop character and charm.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

Chromexcel, the pull-up leather, is very pliable, soft, and maintains high durability. Watch straps achieve a stunning look in no time, which is their best feature.

  • Ages tastefully over time
  • Chromexcel comes in many colors
  • Straps have a unique pattern and texture
  • They are very soft and comfortable

In the photo: Rolex Submariner on Band & Bezel's black Chromexcel leather strap.

What it the average price of a Chromexcel strap?

The price for an Chromexcel watch straps starts at $60 and can go up to $120 and more.

The sweet spot is in the range around $80.
