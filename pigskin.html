Pigskin watch straps

Pigskin leather has a distinctive texture which builds a beautiful patina over time. Watch bands made from this leather are refined, supple and exceptionally durable.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

Pigskin, a leather similar to cowhide in density, has a supple texture and excellent durability. Despite being thinner than cowhide, pigskin remains flexible with use. An excellent choice for a watch strap.

  • They look better with age
  • Pigskin leather is famous for its thinness
  • Soft and comfortable
  • Lightweight on the wrist

    • In the photo: A selection of pigskin straps by Torre Straps.

What it the average price of a pigskin leather strap?

Pigskin straps are not as common as calfskin counterparts, and the average cost is around $80.

The lower-priced options start at around $40, while the premium ones can reach up to $120.
