Canvas nato watch straps

Pass-through straps made from canvas. They are very comfortable to wear and they fit vintage and modern military & sports watches.

Why we love them?

Canvas nato straps are the perfect option for our outdoor activities. Strong fabric material makes them super durable, and since they are of a pass-through construction they are easy to swap out, making it a breeze to switch between different straps.

Plus, they're versatile and can work with a variety of watches, especially field, pilot, divers, or sports.

  • Provide a snug and comfortable fit
  • No break-in period required
  • Watch is secure from a spring bar failure
  • They come in a range of colors and styles

Average price of a canvas nato strap

Canvas nato straps, which are not that common compared to nylon variants, tend to come with a higher price tag, usually averaging around $30.

Luckily, that price is still super affordable, so buy a bunch of them and change them often.
