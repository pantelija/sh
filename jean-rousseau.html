What types of straps does Jean Rousseau sell?

Here is a list of the different types of straps they offer:

Leather Nato

Width: Their bands are made in widths of 18mm, 19mm, 20mm, 21mm, and 22mm. Most of them have the 2mm taper at the buckle side, but 22mm wide straps have the 4mm tapering.

Material: Jean Rousseau straps are made from leather or Cordura canvas.

Their selection of leather types is excellent. Here is a list of the different types of leather material they have available: salmon, sturgeon, buffalo, Cordura, alligator, goat, lizard, plain calf, embossed calf, metallic calf, ostrich, python, alligator, shark, stingray.

They are also proud to offer 100% animal-free straps. The material includes cork, pineapple, and wood.

About Jean Rousseau

Maison Jean Rousseau is a luxury leather goods brand that is distinguished by its creativity, innovation, and cultural heritage. The brand has a rich history of love and passion for beautiful materials and objects, producing not just simple objects, but treasures that will last a lifetime.

Originally specializing in watch straps, the brand has diversified to include a wide range of leather goods, including custom-made pieces, and has expanded its international presence with stores in several countries.

Jean Rousseau's hand-made pieces are created for connoisseurs who value emotion and sensation, with leather as the center of inspiration and creation.
