LIC Leather

LIC has been manufacturing genuine high quality leather since 1971. Brand has become a synonym for straps executed with the know-how and the precision of the master craftsman.

What types of straps does LIC Leather sell?

LIC Leather exclusively offers two-piece leather straps.

Leather Canvas

Width: They offer straps in all widths from 14mm to 26mm. The catalog is pretty big, but luckily, their shop is nicely organized. Visitors can use filters to narrow down the selection by strap width, length, material, color, or price.

Material: LIC Leather uses leather or canvas for their straps. The leather selection is great: alligator, buffalo, Epsom, calfskin, vintage calf, nubuck, and vegan materials.

    • About LIC Leather

    LIC is a family company specializing in high-end leather straps for luxury watches. It was founded in 1971 by Yves Thomas, who had years of experience in luxury leather goods. The company is now being lead by Xavier Thomas and is known for its quality standard, built over 45 years of passion and tradition.

    LIC offers a collection of high-end watch straps for customers looking for refinement and quality in their timepieces.
