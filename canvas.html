Canvas watch straps

Canvas is a strong and breathable material that is comfortable to wear. Canvas watch straps are popular because they are durable, stylish, and versatile.

Why we love them?

Canvas straps can be worn with a variety of different watch styles and are often used to add a laid-back and casual touch to an outfit.

  • They look better with age
  • Last for a long time
  • They are versatile and fit a variety of watches
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • No break-in period required

In the photo: Seiko SNZG13 on Singuar Straps' canvas

Average price of a canvas strap

Canvas straps are usually on the more affordable side. Most prices fit into the range between $15 and $40. But for sure, there are more expensive options available.

Most custom canvas straps are made from premium material and prices for these straps start from $80.
