Leather Custom

About LUGS straps

LUGS is a company started by watch enthusiasts who were frustrated with the lack of high-quality straps and accessories at an acceptable price. They left their corporate jobs to start their own business in order to offer customers true quality at the right price.

Their mission is to provide exceptional quality straps that can change the style of a watch without having to change the watch itself. They use only the finest materials and pay attention to every detail in the crafting process, ensuring unrivaled comfort and luxurious finishing.

Shipping & delivery

LUGS ships worldwide. And the best part: shipping is completely free!

Delivery to the EU and Switzerland usually takes 5-7 days, and to the rest of the world between 7 and 14 days.
