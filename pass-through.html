Pass-through watch straps

Straps made from a single piece of durable (mostly nylon) material that is passed through the spring bars and then secured with a buckle. This design allows the watch to be worn securely on the wrist.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

Military inspired pass-through watch straps are popular because they are strong, durable, and come in fun colors and designs. They are also easy to change, which allows quick and easy switching between different straps.

  • Watch is secure from a spring bar failure
  • They are super strong & durable
  • No break-in period required
  • They come in a range of colors and styles

In the photo: Unimatic UC1 on Singular Straps' Nato Premium

What it the average price of a pass-through strap?

Prices can vary depending on the materials used, the quality of the metal hardware, and the brand.

Natos are generally considered to be an affordable option, and they can often be purchased for between $10 and $30.

Some higher-end nato watch straps may be priced higher, but they usually come from luxury brands and they are not as common.

Common types of pass-through straps

There are several types of military pass-through straps. We could categorize them by color options, by construction, or by the materials used. Here is the most usual way to categorize natos.

  • Straps with heat-sealed keepers
  • Straps with sewn-in keepers
  • Pass-through straps with a floating second keeper
  • Single layer straps (without the second "under keeper")
  • Zulu straps (3-ring or 5-ring construction)
  • Elastic pass-through straps
  • Rubber mil straps
  • Leather pass-through straps
