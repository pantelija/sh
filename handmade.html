Handmade watch straps

Crafted by skilled artisans who use traditional techniques and high-quality materials to create unique watch straps.

Why we love them?

The quality of these straps cannot be matched by typical mass-produced products. Handmade straps are often customized to the individual preferences of the customer.

They are often made from materials such as leather, canvas, or exotic skins, and they may be decorated with stitching, embossing, or other details.

  • Customizable AF
  • Last for a long time
  • Usually one-of-a-kind
  • Made from high-quality materials

Average price of a handmade strap

An entry point for handmade leather straps is $80. From there prices go up fairly quickly.

You are, on average, going to spend between $150 and $200. Some straps can cost several hundred dollars, especially if you need custom endlink fitting, unusual style or you would like your strap to be made from high-end leather or exotic skins.

Handmade nato or elastic straps are below $100.
