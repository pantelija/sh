Suede watch straps

The soft, velvety texture and matte finish of suede result in a refined and elegant watch strap enthusiasts love.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

Suede is a natural material made from the inner side of a hide. It has a soft, velvety surface. Suede straps can be paired with many types of watches, usually ones that have slim cases and that are not top-heavy on the wrist.

  • They comes in many colors
  • Soft and comfortable
  • Add a touch of delicacy and refinement
  • They elevate the look and feel of a watch

In the photo: Seiko SNDC43 on the grey suede strap.

What it the average price of a suede strap?

The price for a suede watch straps starts at $40 and can go up to $120 and more.

An ideal range for getting excellent value and quality sits between $60 and $80.
