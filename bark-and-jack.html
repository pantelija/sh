What types of straps does Bark and Jack sell?

They sell two-piece leather straps and nylon natos.

Nato Leather

Width: Their bands are made in widths of 18mm, 19mm, 20mm, 21mm, and 22mm. Nylon is available in sizes from 19mm to 22mm, and leather is usually 18mm or 20mm.

Material: Bark and Jack straps are either made from leather or nylon. They use calfskin, Shell Cordovan, or vegetable tan leather. When it comes to nylon the options are ribbed, tubular, and seat-belt-like nylon materials.

About Bark and Jack

Bark and Jack is a UK-based company specializing in selling replacement watch straps and watch-related merch. It is a small, family-owned business run by Adrian Barker.

Adrian has been passionately serving the watch community on his YouTube Channel for more than five years. The motto of the company is: "We drink coffee, talk watches, and make awesome products".

The company's goal is to deliver a premium product at an affordable price point, using leather and nylon sourced from renowned manufacturers.

He has a simple sales philosophy of not putting his name on items that he doesn't love and use.
