What types of straps does Hodinkee Shop sell?

Hodinkee Shop offers two-piece leather bands, bund bands, and nylon straps.

Leather Nato Perlon

Width: Their most popular two-piece models are made in 18mm, 20mm, or 22mm widths. Tapering is usually 2mm, but the 22mm straps have a more pronounced taper at the buckle side (22mm/18mm).

If you need a strap for watches with 16mm, 17mm, 19mm or 21mm lug widths, then Hodinkee has you covered, but the selection is not as extensive.

Natos are offered in 20mm and 22mm, and perlons in 18mm, 20mm or 22mm.

Material: All of their two-piece bands are made from leather. Selection is great: calfskin, shell, croc, kangaroo... just to name a few.

In addition to the standard leather, Hodinkee offers animal-free products. These straps are made from fibers of pineapple leaves.

A word about Hodinkee's nylon straps: they are among the best on the market. UTE products, which Hodinkee carries, have nylon that is very soft and super comfortable.

About Hodinkee Shop

Hodinkee is an online resource for wristwatch enthusiasts, established 11 years ago by founder and CEO Benjamin Clymer and his team of writers. They are known for their expertise in historical references and modern haute horlogerie and share their knowledge through various multimedia channels.

They also have a retail platform, the Hodinkee Shop, which launched in 2012 and has grown to become an authorized retailer for over 10 of the most important watch brands in the world.
