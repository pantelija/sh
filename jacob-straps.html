About Jacob Straps

The JacobStraps website offers handmade leather watch straps made with top-quality materials, handcrafted one-by-one using ancient tanning techniques to create unique leather products that give satisfaction and pride to their owners.

They provide care advice for the straps and use only CITES-certified exotic skins of the highest quality. Customers are encouraged to reach out with any questions or suggestions about the products and to enjoy the quality of the handmade leather straps.
