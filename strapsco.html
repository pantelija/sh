About StrapsCo

StrapsCo is a family-run business founded in 2012 by Martin, a watch enthusiast who was unhappy with the high cost and limited options for replacement straps for his Breitling Navitimer. Martin approached manufacturers and began creating his own straps, which he then sold at a reasonable price.

StrapsCo's goal is to provide high-quality watch bands at a reasonable price, making it easy and enjoyable to change the look of a watch. They believe that every watch — no matter its size, shape, or design — has a story to tell, and it is the strap that pulls it all together.

Changing straps on your watch is more cost-effective than purchasing multiple watches. The focus is on providing a satisfying experience for those looking to switch up the style of their classic or smartwatch, or revitalize an older timepiece.
