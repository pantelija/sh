What types of straps does Mill Handmade sell?

They produce two-piece leather straps. Nothing more, nothing less.

Leather Elastic Canvas Custom

Width: They are usually creating bespoke straps. Customers can specify both the width and the desired length.

Mill Handmade provides buckles with their straps. Please keep in mind that straps that have odd buckle widths (e.g. 17mm) will not include a buckle.

Material: Mill Handmade uses a variety of leather types, including calfskin, Shell Cordovan, Buttero, Pueblo, Epsom, ostrich, and more.

All leathers are sourced from the finest tanneries around the world known for their high standards.

About Mill Handmade

Mill Handmade is a small leather crafting workshop from Brisbane, Australia. They create watch straps using traditional leather crafting techniques.

Each product is meticulously cut, saddle stitched, and burnished by hand. They source our leathers from some of the world's best tanneries, using only full-grain leather that showcases its inherent characteristics and displays slight variations in every hide.
