About Watch Strap Heaven

The company specializes in creating high-quality, handcrafted watch straps. Jan, the founder of the company developed a fascination for watches at a young age when he inherited an old Lanco pocket watch. This sparked his interest in watches.

Team at Watch Strap Heaven believes that a watch can only truly fit the wrist with an excellent strap. All the straps offered by the company are 100% handmade, with no machines used in the manufacturing process.

The materials used for the straps are carefully selected, with many of them sourced from Europe and the USA. For example, the Horween leather, known for its quality, comes from Chicago, while the Vintage and Suede series are made using the finest leathers from the UK, Italy, Japan, and Slovakia.
