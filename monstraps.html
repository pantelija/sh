About Monstraps

Monstraps is a Singapore-based company that offers high-quality watch straps with comfort as their main priority. They have a strong focus on research and development, and are involved in every step of the process from selecting leather hides to precision finishing.

They provide replacement bands for a wide range of watch brands (Rolex, Tudor, Omega, Longines, IWC, Breitling, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet..), and are committed to providing excellent service and quality to watch collectors.
