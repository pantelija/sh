What types of straps does Crafter Blue sell?

Crafter Blue produces two-piece rubber and leather straps.

Rubber Leather

Width: They offer straight-end or curved-end rubber straps that fit the most popular watches from Tudor, Seiko, and Rolex. In addition, they sell universal straight-end straps in 20mm and 22mm widths.

Material: Their straps are made from vulcanized rubber. Vulcanization improves the properties of the rubber by increasing its strength, flexibility, and resistance to heat and other environmental factors.

About Crafter Blue

Crafter Blue is a producer of dive watches and straps for free divers, sea photographers, and oceanographers. They create products that are stylish, high-quality, and designed to support sea exploration. Their mission is to support oceanography by creating watches and accessories that help people live adventurous.

The company also sponsors oceanography projects and collaborates with diver groups to share their adventures with the world through their YouTube channel.
