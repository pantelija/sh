What types of straps does Band & Bezel sell?

Band & Bezel offers two-piece leather straps and pass-through nato straps.

Pass-through Leather

Width: They offer straps that are suitable for watches that have lug spacing of 18mm, 20mm, and 22mm.

Leather two-piece and standard nato straps are available in all mentioned sizes. Seatbelt and ribbed nato straps are offered in 20mm and 22mm.

Tapering on 18mm leather straps is 2mm. The 20mm and 22mm leather straps have a more pronounced taper at the buckle side (22mm/18mm and 20mm/16mm).

Material: Band & Bezel straps are either made from leather or nylon. Their leather is sourced from the finest tanneries in France, and includes Chromexcel, Martino, and Vax veg leather.

When it comes to pass-through straps they offer various types of nylon: standard, seatbelt-like, and ribbed.

About Band & Bezel

Band & Bezel is a watch strap company that offers customized designs with premium quality at non-discouraging prices. They are committed to delivering the highest quality watch straps that enhance the functionality of your timepiece while serving as a personal expression of style and personality.

They began their journey with simple watches and gradually learned to appreciate quality and their unique features. They realized that even small details can add value to a watch and started thinking of ways to improve their own collections.

Their straps are designed in Canada with raw materials sourced from the finest tanneries in France and manufactured in Asia. They strive to provide the best straps for both casual and seasoned collectors, balancing luxury and practicality.
