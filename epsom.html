Epsom leather watch straps

Lightweight calf leather straps with a semi-matte, pressed cross-hatch texture that has a charming appearance.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

The aesthetic appeal is the reason why Epsom leather straps are popular. Simply said, they look cool and can be paired with many types of watches. As a material, Epsom scratches less compared to other types of leather, and easily conforms to the shape of the wrist over time. A great choice for a watch band.

  • Lightweight on the wrist
  • Less likely to show scratches
  • Easy to maintain
  • Straps hold their shape over time

What it the average price of an Epsom watch strap?

The price for an Epsom watch straps starts at $40 and can go up to $150 and more.

The sweet spot is in the range between $80 and $110.
