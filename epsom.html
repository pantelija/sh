Epsom leather watch straps

Lightweight calf leather straps with a semi-matte, pressed cross-hatch texture that has a charming appearance.

Why we love them?

The appeal of Epsom leather straps lies in their aesthetic appeal, particularly as they scratch less compared to other types of leather. Material easily conforms to the shape of the wrist over time, therefore it is a perfect choice for a watch band. Also, Epsom straps are can be paired with many types of watches.

  • Lightweight on the wrist
  • Less likely to show scratches
  • Easy to maintain
  • Straps hold their shape over time

Average price of an Epsom watch strap

The price for an Epsom watch straps starts at $40 and can go up to $150 and more.

The sweet spot is in the range between $80 and $110.
