What types of straps does Torre Straps sell?

They create two-piece leather straps, bund watch bands, and leather pass-through straps.

Leather Custom Pass-through

Width: Torre Straps' owner, Matteo, offers custom services for watch straps. The standard range includes sizes between 14mm to 24mm, but if you require a specific dimension, you can request a custom order, and Matteo will be happy to make a strap to your specifications.

Additionally, Matteo crafts open-ended watch straps that are suitable for watches with fixed spring bars and closed lugs.

Material: Torre Straps exclusively uses leather of the highest quality. All types of leather are sourced from the finest tanneries around the world known for their high standards.

Here is a list of the different types of leather material Matteo has available: calfskin, pigskin, goatskin, alligator, crocodile, Epsom, Saffiano, Shell Cordovan.

Finishing can be smooth or grainy. Of course leather is available in either matte or shinny color options.

Collections

They do not have ongoing & ready-to-wear strap collections, since they are primarly doing custom work.

About Torre Straps

Torre Straps is not just another watch strap company. Matteo, the owner, is a true artisan who is investing a lot of time and dedication to create his products.

He invested a significant amount of time and dedication into studying old straps, purchasing hundreds of them to perfect the process, materials, and details. It is rare to find an artisan who has such a big collection of straps from the '30s and '40s.

Today he uses traditional techniques to handcraft his bands, including hand stitching and finishing. In the current market that is filled with numerous options, Torre Straps stand out due to their unique and exceptional style.

As a result, Torre Strap is one of the best places to get the correct and accurate replacement band for your vintage watch. He crafts a watch strap that accurately reflects the characteristics and attributes typical of the historical period to which the watch belongs to. The perfect strap one for your vintage watch.
