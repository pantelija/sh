What types of straps does STRAPosphere sell?

Here is the list of strap types they offer:

Width: They offer straps that are suitable for watches that have lug spacing of 18mm, 20mm, and 22mm. Rubber and pass-through bands are made in 20mm or 22mm, and leather ones also include 18mm models.

About STRAPosphere

STRAPosphere is a new watch strap company started by two cousins who share a passion for watches and straps. Based in Singapore, they believe in providing high-quality and comfortable straps at affordable prices.

They cater to all types of customers, regardless of whether they own one watch or multiple, and have a wide range of products to suit different styles and needs.

Reliability, quality, functionality and affordability are key values that we try to translate into our brand and products.
