Calfskin watch straps

Calfskin straps offer comfort, a great look, and they can go with nearly any watch style. They come in all price ranges and they are readily available.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

Calfskin is the most common material used for making a watch strap. These straps look great, especially after a few months of wear when they conform to the shape of the wrist. Plus they complement a variety of outfits and types of watches.

  • Big selection in almost any price range
  • Unmatched number of patterns and finishes
  • They look better with age
  • Good ones are very comfortable
  • Stylistically they suit almost any watch

In the photo: Heuer Carrera on a calfskin strap by Band & Bezel.

What it the average price of a calfskin strap?

The price of a calfskin watch strap can vary widely depending on the grain of leather & the brand. Affordable straps are priced between $20 and $50, really good ones from $50 to $80, and high-qualily straps start from $80.

An entry point for full-grain handmade stuff is usually at $80, but be aware that prices go up fairly quickly from there.
