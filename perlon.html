Perlon watch straps

Super comfy pass-through straps with unlimited adjustability since they do not have pre-set holes.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

Perlons usually have a braided design, which adds to their unique appearance and makes them stand out.

  • Lightweight on the wrist
  • Soft and comfortable
  • Tons of color options
  • Breathable
  • Easy to nail a perfect fit

In the photo: Seiko SNDC43 on Crown & Buckle's perlon

What it the average price of a perlon strap?

Perlons are quite affordable straps. They usually fall into the range between $10 and $20. Buy a bunch of them and have fun!
