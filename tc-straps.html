What types of straps does TC Straps sell?

Here is the list of strap types they offer:

Leather Canvas Custom

Width: The company offers replacement straps in 22mm, 24mm, or 26mm. Straps that are 24 wide are suitable for Panerai watches that have case sizes of 44mm, and 26mm bands fit 45mm and 47mm Panerai models.

Material: TC's straps are made from leather of the highest quality. They use alligator, sharkskin, crocodile, and ostrich leg leather to name several options. They also use unique, exotic skins that are rarely seen on the market.

In addition to leather straps, TC Straps offer collections made out of canvas, Cordura®, and Carbon fiber.

About TC Straps

TC Straps is a company that specializes in replacement straps and bands for Panerai watches. They are the #1 seller of straps for Panerai watches in the world, and they have over 2000 straps in stock at all times.

The straps are made in Italy at the Original Panerai straps factory. TC Straps also distributes various brands including Mario Paci Watches and Straps, Original Kodiak Waterproof Straps, TC Vintage straps, Lady TC, Giovanni e Figlio, Toscana Straps.

TC Strps own the Viapaneristi Watch talk forum, a specialized location for Panerai watches. The company also sells accessories for Panerai watch enthusiasts, including cases and buckles.
