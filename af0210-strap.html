What types of straps does A.F.0210. Strap sell?

Here is a list of the different types of straps they offer:

Nato

Width: Their models are made in widths of 16mm, 17.5mm, 18mm, 20mm, and 22mm. Straps respect the original military specification but have slight modifications to accommodate modern watches.

Material: A.F.0210. uses nylon webbing to make their straps. Straps are paired with either non-magnetic brass buckles, plated matt black buckles, or standard stainless steel ones.
