What types of straps does BluShark Straps sell?

Here is the list of strap types they offer:

Nato Leather Elastic Rubber Canvas

Width: Their most popular models are made in 18mm, 20mm, or 22mm widths. Most of them have the 2mm taper at the buckle side.

In addition, there are quite a few collections in both pass-through and two-piece styles that offer straps in 24mm.

Material: BluShark uses nylon, canvas, Cordura® fabric, rubber, and leather to produce their straps. They have various types of nylon: standard, seat-belt like, w-weave, knit-weave, and elastic, just to list a few.
