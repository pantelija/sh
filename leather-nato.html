Leather nato straps

Sometimes you don't care about performance, strenght, water resistance, or extreme durability. Sometimes you just want to look cool. A lether nato does the trick.

Why we love them?

Leather nato straps are a great option when we want to enjoy the benefits of a pass-through strap while enhancing the look of watches and elevating our outfit and appearance.

  • No break-in period required
  • They are more comfortable compared to nylon options
  • They elevate the look and feel of a watch
  • Watch is secure from a spring bar failure

Average price of a leather nato strap

Prices vary depending on the quality of the leather materials used, the quality of the metal hardware, and the brand.

An average price of a quality leather nato is $60.

Higher-end options start from $100. They are usually made from exotic skins, shell cordovan, full-grain, or top-grain calfskin leather.
