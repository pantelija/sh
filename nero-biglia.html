What types of straps does Nero Biglia sell?

Nero Biglia produces two-piece leather straps.

Leather Custom

Width: Their leather bands are made in 18mm, 19mm, or 20mm widths. Most of them have the 2mm taper at the buckle side.

Material: This strap are crafted from top grain vegetable tanned leather, which is known for its superior resistance and longevity. When it comes to finishing, they offer both smooth and grained calfskin leather.

About Nero Biglia

Nero Biglia was founded with the goal of providing a high-quality and long-lasting product. They are environmentally conscious and use vegetable-tanned leather and reusable packaging.

They aim to enhance the customer experience through practicality and personalization, offering a quick release system for easy strap switching and customization services like custom strap sizing and engraving. They also offer the option for regular spring bars for those who prefer a traditional installation process.
