Nato pass-through watch straps

Straps made from a single piece of durable (mostly nylon) material that is passed through the spring bars and then secured with a buckle. This design allows the watch to be worn securely on the wrist.

Why we love them?

Nato watch straps are popular because they are strong, durable, and come in fun colors and designs. They are also easy to change, which allows quick and easy switching between different straps.

  • Watch is secure from a spring bar failure
  • They are super strong & durable
  • No break-in period required
  • They come in a range of colors and styles

Average price of a nato strap

Prices can vary depending on the materials used, the quality of the metal hardware, and the brand.

Nato watch straps are generally considered to be an affordable option, and they can often be purchased for between $10 and $30.

Some higher-end NATO watch straps may be priced higher, but they usually come from luxury brands and they are not as common.

Common types of natos

There are several types of nato straps. We could categorize them by color options, by construction, or by the materials used. Here is the most usual way to categorize natos.

  • Nato straps with heat-sealed keepers
  • Nato straps with sewn-in keepers
  • Nato straps with a floating second keeper
  • Single layer nato straps (without the second "under keeper")
  • Zulu straps (3-ring or 5-ring construction)
  • Elastic nato straps
  • Rubber nato straps
  • Leather nato straps
