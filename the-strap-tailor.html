About The Strap Tailor

David, owner & founder, is a person who has a passion for horology, which was passed on to him by his grandfather. He inherited first watch from him and has been fascinated by the mechanics and lasting nature of watches ever since.

He now runs a business that sells luxury and bespoke watch straps all over the world. The Strap Tailor specializes in leatherwork and uses materials such as ostrich, French calfskin, Epsom calf, goat skin, pig skin, and suede to make their straps.
