About Vecchio Times

The owner of the company started a private collection of watches in 2001. He soon realized that product availability for vintage watch accessories was limited in Australia, so he created his own range of straps and bands.

Today Vecchio Times offers products that are made in Italy using traditional techniques and treated with natural elements to achieve a unique vintage look and feel.
