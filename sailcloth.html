Sailcloth watch straps

Sailcloth is a durable and water-resistant fabric that is used in the construction of sails for boats. When used in watches it creates a long-lasting and stylish product.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

Sailcloth watch straps are known for their toughness and resistance to water, making them a popular choice for sports watches and other watches that may be exposed to water or other elements.

  • They are fully waterproof
  • More formal than rubber
  • More versatile than leather
  • Lightweight and flexible straps
  • Stylistically they suit almost any watch

What it the average price of a sailcloth strap?

The price range for sailcloth watch straps varies widely, from around $25 to $90. It depends on the brand, quality, and design of the strap.

The average price is $55, and the median is $40 (expensive ones push the average away from the median value).

For premium options please check out Artem's sailcloth. They have a variety of styles and prices start from $85.
