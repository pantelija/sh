About RSM Watch Strap

RSM Watch Strap is a company based in Singapore that specializes in designing and producing high-quality watch straps. The company was founded by a group of architects and designers who shared a passion for good watch straps. Living in Singapore, they experienced the challenges of hot and humid weather, which motivated them to create watch straps that could withstand such conditions.

The founders of RSM Watch Strap were inspired by an episode of the Netflix series "Our Planet," where the impact of climate change was highlighted through an encounter between Sophie Lanfear and a walrus. This encounter made them realize the importance of taking action to protect the environment. They aimed to build a business that not only offered excellent products but also raised awareness among customers about their individual impact on the environment.

The company's logo, featuring the outline of a walrus, serves as a reminder for customers to make small changes in their lives that contribute to a larger positive impact on the world. RSM Watch Strap is committed to sourcing raw materials in the most efficient and environmentally friendly way possible.

Initially, the company provided woven watch straps, but they expanded their product range to include leather, silk, and synthetic straps.
