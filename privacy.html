Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting our website.

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit www.straphunter.com.

Tracking

In order to minimize the collection of personal information when you visit our website, we have opted for Lite Analytics as our website analytics tool. Lite Analytics is compliant with various privacy regulations such as GDPR, ePrivacy (including PECR), COPPA, and CCPA, and does not rely on cookies. By utilizing this privacy-conscious website analytics software, we only briefly process your IP address. We, the website operators, are not able to identify you. Under the CCPA, your personal information is rendered unidentifiable.

Our intention in using this software is to gain insights into our website traffic while prioritizing privacy, enabling us to consistently enhance our website and business.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from you when you visit our website. We only collect some non-personally identifiable information (non-PII) which is collected automatically through our website hosting service and Lite Analytics, and is used solely for website analytics and to improve our website's performance.

As a visitor to the straphunter.com website

As a visitor to the Site:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No information such as cookies is stored
  • No information is shared with, sent to or sold to third-parties
  • No information is shared with advertising companies

Use of Cookies

We do not use cookies on our website. Therefore, you can be confident that your visits to our website are anonymous and that we do not collect any personal information about you.

Visitors' submissions

Visitors to our website can send suggestions for watch strap stores that they would like to see added to our catalog.

When submitting a suggestion, we will ask for an email address so that we can contact you if we need more information about the store you are suggesting or to let you know if we have added the store to our catalog.

Please note that we will only use your email address for correspondence related to your suggestion. We will not use your email address for marketing purposes or share it with any third parties.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share any of your information with third parties, and we do not sell or rent any information to anyone. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. If we make any material changes to this policy, we will notify you by posting an updated version on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us at info@straphunter.com.

Thank you for visiting our website.
