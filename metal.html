Metal bracelets and bands

Metal bracelets are super comfortable. They can be adjusted to fit the wrist which is especially useful for larger watches, which can feel uncomfortable if they are not properly supported by the band.

Why we love them?

Metal bracelets are available in a wide range of styles, from classic and elegant to modern and sporty. This makes them a versatile option for a variety of occasions.

  • They are durable and long-lasting
  • Good ones are very comfortable
  • Can be worn with a variety of watches
  • No break-in period required

Average prices of metal bands & bracelet

The price range for metal bracelets varies widely, from around $40 to $150+. It depends on the brand, quality, and design of the band.

Replacement metal bands with solid end-links for the most popular brands and models (i.e. Seiko Omega, Hamilton, Rolex...) can be found for as little as $70.
