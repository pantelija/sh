What types of straps do ColaReb® sell?

ColaReb® produces two-piece leather straps.

Leather

Width: Their most popular models are made in 18mm, 20mm, or 22mm widths. Most of them have the 2mm taper at the buckle side.

If you need a strap for watches with 16mm, 17mm, 19mm or 21mm lug widths, then ColaReb® got you covered, but the selection is not as extensive.

Material: All of their straps are made from leather. In addition to the standard leather, ColaReb® is proud to offer animal-free products. These straps are made from microfiber, wooden fiber, paper fiber or apple skin.

What makes them special?

Their straps are so cool! Leather straps can be quite pretentious and formal and ColaReb® cleverly avoids that.

Most of their straps have a specific aged texture and natural patina. This brings a unique character and a classic Italian effortless style to any outfit.

About ColaReb®

ColaReb® is a company that produces unique leather watch straps and cases that are handcrafted in Italy by leather craftsmen from the Marche and Veneto regions. They use traditional techniques from the Renaissance era to create high quality products that are conscious choices for consumers who value quality and craftsmanship.

ColaReb® carefully designs and creates all the details of their straps, making each one unique and able to harmonize with any watch. The company aims to represent the rich history and tradition of Italian leather craftsmanship around the world.
