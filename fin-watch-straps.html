About Fin Watch Straps

FinWatchStraps is a family-owned company based in Helsinki, Finland that specializes in hand-made leather watch straps. They use high-quality, eco-friendly materials and all products are crafted by hand.

They offer customizable straps to match clients' unique personalities and work with them to create a design that suits their preferences and watch brands. They use only the best materials and communicate closely with clients to ensure a perfect final product.
