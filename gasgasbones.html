About GasGasBones

GasGasBones will no longer produce watch straps on a full time basis.

Carl, the GGB owner joined the Royal Air Force as a Survival Equipment Fitter and did nearly 25 years of service. After his service ended, he became interested in watches and started making the SPV3 watch. He then designed the SPV1 watch strap, which became the best-selling product in his lineup.

Carl started selling these straps on eBay and eventually decided to try GGB full-time in 2012. The GasGasBones brand has evolved over the years, and the company is now involved in a variety of projects. Today GasGasBones has a kind of cult following and a super supportive and passionate customer base.
