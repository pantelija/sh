What types of straps does Clockwork Republic sell?

They exclusively produce and sell rubber straps:

Rubber

Width: Their most popular straps are made in the width of 22mm and they are suitable for Seiko watches (SKX, Samurai, Sports, Turtle). Most bands have the 2mm taper at the buckle side.

Material: Clockwork Republic sells FKM straps. FKM, also known as fluoroelastomer, is a top-quality rubber material commonly used in the aerospace industry. FKM has hypoallergenic properties, it is resistant to solvents, chemicals, and high temperatures.

Despite being soft and flexible, FKM is tough and durable, making it capable of withstanding demanding and extreme conditions.

About Clockwork Republic

Clockwork Republic is a company founded in 2020 by watch enthusiasts who were passionate about finding the perfect watch straps. They discovered integrated straps and loved their seamless appearance, but were disappointed by the limited options available, which were mostly designed for luxury watches.

Recognizing that other watch enthusiasts shared their frustration, Clockwork Republic set out on a mission to provide high-quality integrated straps that were accessible and affordable. They believe that premium straps shouldn't have to be expensive.

One of their notable products is the End Link Rubber Strap, which features a unique steel end-link design connected to a pair of premium rubber straps. This design seamlessly integrates with the watch. The rubber straps are made from FKM, known for its softness, suppleness, and durability in harsh environments.
