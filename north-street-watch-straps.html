About North Street Watch Straps

North Street Watch began as a passion project for watches, nature, architecture, and beautifully made things. They focus on creating straps that are not only beautiful in the finished product but also in the hand-craftsmanship that goes into each one.

They use an old-world manufacturing process and are proud to share their story and artisanal work. They are humbled to earn customers' business and a place on their special watch.
