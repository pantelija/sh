Lizard leather watch straps

Lizard skin straps have a unique pattern, with scales that are smaller than crocodile's or alligator's. Also, these exotic straps are well known for their flexibility.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

Lizard skin watch straps stand out. People will notice you. These straps are not laid back or casual, but they elevate the appearance of your watch, and your overall outfit.

Do not worry, they are not over the top. They do not scream "look at me", but offer a stylish and sophisticated touch.

  • Straps are supple, silky, and very flexible
  • Elegant and sophisticated look
  • They have a distinctive scaly texture
  • They look awesome in bold colors

What it the average price of a lizard skin strap?

Lizard straps tend to cost more than other leather bands as a result of the scarcity of the material and the time-consuming process of making the straps.

They are a premium product and fit in the price range of other exotic leather types as crocodile or alligator.

On average, lizard skin straps cost from $160 to $180. It is worth noting that there are both more budget-friendly and pricier options available in the market.
