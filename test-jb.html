What types of straps does Joseph Bonnie sell?

Here is the list of strap types they offer:

Leather Nato Metal Rubber

Width: Their two-piece bands are made in 18mm, 19mm, 20mm, 21, or 22mm sizes. Most of them have the 2mm taper at the buckle side.

Most of the Joseph Bonnie's pass-through straps are in 18mm or 20mm, but there are few options available in 19mm and 22mm.

Material: Joseph Bonnie uses a variety of leather types, including calfskin, cordovan, pigskin, or exotic skins. In addition, they have straps made out of rubber, and few a steel models (mesh & Bonklip).

About Joseph Bonnie

A team based in Paris designs and produces fashion creations under the name Joseph Bonnie. They prioritize high-quality craftsmanship.

While the team members were working with an independent online magazine Les Rhabilleurs they gained lots of experience and they trained themselves to have an eye for aesthetics and good design.

In 2014. Joseph Bonnie started offering products designed in Paris and made by skilled partners.
