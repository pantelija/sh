About PhenomeNato Straps

PhenomeNato started developing premium seatbelt type nato straps in 2015 to compete with Swiss brands in terms of quality while being more affordable. They continue to manufacture high-quality nato straps in the EU. Nowadays, this is a rare occasion since many brands simply redistribute logo-branded straps from China.
