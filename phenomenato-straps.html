What types of straps does Phenome Straps sell?

Phenome Straps produces and sells nylon nato straps.

Pass-through

Width: All their straps are made in 18mm, 20mm, or 22mm widths.

Material: Phenome Straps' bands are made from a smooth nylon webbing that is finely woven, paired with stainless-steel rectangular keepers and a sewn-in, secure buckle.

About Phenome Straps

Phenome Straps started developing premium seatbelt type nato straps in 2015 to compete with Swiss brands in terms of quality while being more affordable. They continue to manufacture high-quality nato straps in the EU. Nowadays, this is a rare occasion since many brands simply redistribute logo-branded straps from China.
