Zulu straps

Zulu strap is an upgraded and sturdier variation of the typical nato strap, featuring a longer length, thicker nylon, and heavy-duty steel hardware for improved durability.

Why we love them?

When maximum water resistance, a secure fit, extra length, and excellent watch protection are required, then we usually grab a zulu strap for the task ahead.

  • Last for a long time
  • Can be worn over the outer layer of clothes
  • Watch is secure from a spring bar failure
  • Tons of color options

Average price of a zulu strap

Zulu watch straps are a budget-friendly option, typically available for a price range of $10 to $30.

More expensive zulus are usually made from leather (i.e Shell Cordovan) and prices for these bands start from $100.
