Crocodile leather watch straps

Genuine crocodile leather watch straps are known for their supreme elegance and unique pattern. They are premium products that are usually paired with luxury watches.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

Firstly, this page showcases retailers and artisans that offer straps made from a genuine crocodile leather.

To the contrary, an embossed crocodile (or croco) is a type of leather, usually made from calfskin, that has been treated to have a crocodile-like texture imprinted onto its surface.

Retailers that work with the genuine leather must comply to the CITES regulation, an international agreement aimed at ensuring that international trade in specimens of wild animals and plants does not threaten their survival.

Having outlined that, let us now mention some of the advantages of using crocodile leather straps.

  • They have a unique scaly texture
  • Crocodile leather is soft and supple to the touch
  • One of the most elegant watch bands
  • Very comfortable

What it the average price of a crocodile leather strap?

Crocodile leather straps are usually pricier than other leather bands due to the scarcity of the material and the labor-intensive process of tanning and creating the straps. The cost varies depending on factors such as the brand, design, and manual work involved.

Prices for crocodile straps can start from $150, with an average range between $190 and $250.
