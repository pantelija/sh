Shell Cordovan watch straps

A smooth, durable leather with a glossy finish and a tight grain structure. Shell Cordovan straps develop a rich patina over time, maintain top finish, and rarely crease.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

Shell Cordovan straps stand out. They are not as flashy as exotic skins, or casual as suede or nubuck. Cordovan has a rich color vibrancy and a perfectly smooth top finish. Lovely straps.

Cordovan leather is made from horsehide. Only the specific parts of the horse, to be exact. That part is the "shell" or the rear quarter of the animal.

The reason this specific part is used is due to its dense fiber structure, which provides the leather with its well-known durability and strength. Such a combination of features makes this type of leather an ideal material for a watch strap.

  • Shell cordovan is very durable
  • Leather has an elegant & smooth top finish
  • Straps are resistant to creasing
  • They look better with age

What it the average price of a Shell Cordovan leather strap?

Shell Cordovan straps are not as common as calfskin options and this affects their prices. They tend to be more expensive with an average cost of $130.

An entry point is at $100, while the premium ones easily exceed $250.
