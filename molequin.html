What types of straps does Molequin Straps sell?

Molequin produces two-piece leather straps. Nothing more, nothing less.

Leather Custom

Width: They offer straps for watches with 17mm, 18mm, 19mm, 20mm, 21mm, or 22mm lug widths. And the best part: almost all collections have straps in every single one of the listed sizes.

Please keep in mind that their straps have a pronounced taper. Here are the measurements (lug size/buckle size):

  • 18mm/16mm

About Molequin Straps

Molequin is a leather goods brand that was founded in 2016 and is based on three principles: using only ethically-sourced leather, producing products to the highest standards, and promoting a timeless style rather than fleeting fads. The company's founders, Michael and Sidney, have a vision of bridging the gap between Anglo-French cultures and generations.

The company is based in France and draws on the country's rich history of leather craftsmanship. Quality and craftsmanship are emphasized in their production.
