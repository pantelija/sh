Single pass watch straps

Single pass straps have a similar construction as regular nato straps, but lack the second under keeper to create a clean and streamlined look.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

Single pass straps are popular because they are durable, easy to change, and come in many different materials and styles.

  • Less bulk on the wrist
  • They create a clean and streamlined look
  • Watch is secure from a spring bar failure
  • They are super strong & durable

In the photo: Unimatic UC1 on Singular Straps' Nato Premium

What it the average price of a single pass watch strap?

Nylon or canvas single pass straps are generally considered to be an affordable option, and they can often be purchased for between $15 and $30.

Leather single pass straps start at $70, and can go up to $160 for exotic skins.
