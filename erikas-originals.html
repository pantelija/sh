About Erika's Originals

The Marine Nationale watch strap was created by French naval divers in the late 1960s/early 1970s for their Tudor MN watches. Strap was made from a flexible woven elastic and parachuting hardware and is designed to fit over a wetsuit sleeve and withstand salt water exposure.

Erika's Originals' patented MN™ strap is based on this design and is available in various colors and widths.
