Mesh watch bands

Flexible and comfortable watch bands made of interlocking stainless steel loops or rings.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

Mesh metal watch bracelets are lightweight, breathable, adjustable, easy to maintain, affordable, and hypoallergenic. They also offer an excellent value for money. What more to ask for?

  • Provide more breathability compared to other steel options
  • The lightest of all metal bands
  • Sliding fold-over clasp enables the perfect adjustability

In the photo: Seiko SNGZ13J on a mesh bracelet.

Average prices of mesh bands & bracelets?

The cost of mesh bracelets can fluctuate greatly, ranging from approximately $40 to over $200. This depends on the specific brand, the level of quality, and the design of the bracelet.

On average, mesh tends to be priced around $80.
