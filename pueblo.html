Pueblo leather watch straps

Pueblo straps have an attractive appeal due to their soft, supple texture and matte finish. They bring different esthetics and add a touch of delicacy and refinement.

Why we love them?

The combination of a matte finish and slight graininess results in a refined appearance. Pueblo bands are a good choice for those who appreciate a sophisticated yet understated style.

  • They look better with age
  • Add a touch of delicacy and refinement
  • Soft and comfortable
  • Italian full-grain leather
