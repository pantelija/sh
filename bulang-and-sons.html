About Bulang and Sons

Bulang and Sons is a company that caters to watch lovers who appreciate timeless quality and understated style. The collection is curated by Bernhard Bulang, who personally designs or hand-picks every item.

Bulang and Sons values long-lasting relationships and superior service, and only sells products that the team would want to buy or collect themselves.

The company offers a subtle blend of style and quality, with a focus on vintage masterpieces and exclusive leather watch straps. Bulang and Sons encourages customers to share their own pictures, thoughts and style statements using the hashtag #TeamBulang.
