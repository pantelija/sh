About The House Of Straps

The company specializes in crafting leather watch straps made from leathers sourced from the US, Italy, and France. Operating on a made-to-order model, each strap is carefully handmade in their Singapore studio, ensuring attention to detail and top-notch quality.

Made-to-Order straps have a lead time of 15 days, while Ready-Made straps are available for shipping within 3 days of ordering.
