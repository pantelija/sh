What types of straps does REM Straps sell?

Here is the list of strap types they offer:

Leather Custom

Width: The majority of straps are created to fit watches that have a lug width of 20mm. They also have a few products available in 19mm, 21mm, or 22mm.

For example, IWC specific straps come in 20mm and 21mm. Similarly, straps for the Omega Speedmaster can be found in 19mm or 20mm.

Material: REM Straps uses leather of the highest quality. Leather is vegetable tanned and comes from Tärnsjö Garveri, a well-respected Swedish tannery.

About REM Straps

Totte el Siddig and Roger Clementz, watch enthusiasts, met at a forum and discovered their shared belief that watch bands could be elevated with storytelling. They created the concept of REM watch straps, which are locally made in Sweden. Totte has a background in design and art direction, while Roger is an engineer.

The motto of the company is best described with a statement: "You should be able to select a strap that highlights the historical significance of your watch."
