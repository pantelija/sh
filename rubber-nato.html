Rubber nato watch straps

An upgraded variation of a nato strap that brings a soft and flexible fit, great durability, resistance to external elements, and uncompromised performance.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

The choice between rubber, canvas, or nylon nato straps ultimately depends on personal preference, but in general rubber natos are a good option when you need maximum water resistance, a snug fit around your wrist, and superb watch security.

  • They do not stretch out when wet
  • Rubber natos dry instantly
  • Chemical, UV, and salt resistant
  • Comfortable straps, of course

In the photo: Unimatic UC-1 on the Singular Straps' Nato Gummi.

What it the average price of a rubber nato strap?

Rubber natos, which are less common than nylon counterparts, tend to have a higher cost, with the average price being just under $50.
