What types of straps does Threaded Leather Co sell?

The company produces two-piece leather bands and nylon straps.

Leather Pass-through Custom

Width: Their most popular leather models are made in widths of 18mm, 20mm, 22mm, and 24mm. Straps follow a standard sizing of 125mm/80mm, and the tapering is 2mm.

Nylon straps only come in 20mm and 22mm, and of course they do not taper.

Material: Threaded Leather Co uses the finest full-grain leathers sourced from Horween and Hermann Oak tanneries. When it comes to pass-through straps they use a seatbelt-like nylon blend.

About Threaded Leather Co

The company was founded in the summer of 2015 in Arlington, Virginia. James, the founder, started the venture after creating a unique strap for his Seiko SKX007. Motivated by the exceptional results and the quality of Horween Leather, he is dedicated to providing the world with top-notch watch straps through Threaded Leather.
