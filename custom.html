Custom watch straps

Many watch lovers choose to order custom watch straps to give their watch a more unique or personalized look, or to replace a damaged or worn-out original strap.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

We love them because they can be customized with different colors, patterns, or other design elements to suit our personal style.

  • Made to the specifications
  • Customizable AF
  • Usually one-of-a-kind
  • Made from high-quality materials
  • Sometimes they are the only option for a replacement band

In the photo: Seiko SNDC43 on the Pugo Straps' calfskin band

What it the average price of a custom strap?

An entry point for custom leather straps is $60.

Some straps can cost several hundred dollars, especially if you need custom endlink fitting, or you would like your strap to be made from top-grade material.

Custom nato, elastic, or canvas straps are below $100.
