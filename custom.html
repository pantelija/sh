Custom watch straps

Many watch lovers choose to order a custom watch straps to give their watch a more unique or personalized look, or to replace a damaged or worn-out original strap.

We we love them?

We love them because they can be customized with different colors, patterns, or other design elements to suit our personal style.

  • Made to the specifications
  • Customizable AF
  • Usually one-of-a-kind
  • Made from high-quality materials
  • Sometimes they are the only option for a replacement band

Average price of a custom strap

Entry point for a custom leather straps is at $60.

Some straps can cost several hundred dollars, especially of you need custom endlink fitting, or you would like your strap to be made from top-grade material.

Custom nato, elastic or canvas straps are below $100.
