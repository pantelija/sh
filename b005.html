How to use adapters on G-Shock watches to install nato straps?

One thing that G-Shock watches traditionally lacked was the ability to easily swap out straps. That's where adapters come in.

G-Shock watches are exceptionally durable and tough. They were designed to withstand extreme conditions. They followed "form follows function" mantra; design was all about making them indestructible.

But soon after the initial release, Casio realized that G-Shocks shouldn't remain conservative and plain-looking; they quickly became super cool and stylish. Casio started introducing different colorways, patterns, pop-culture references... you name it.

However, one thing that G-Shock watches traditionally lacked was the ability to easily swap out straps.

That's where adapters come in. Adapters are small metal or plastic pieces that attach to the lugs, allowing you to install pass-through straps that come in many different styles and colors.

Why bother with pass-through straps?

There are many benefits of pass-through straps. First, they offer a secure fit. Even if one spring bar fails, the watch will stay on your wrist. Honestly, even if your G-Shock hits the ground, it will be fine, but let's not ignore this benefit of a Nato or Zulu strap.

Natos and other pass-through straps also allow for easy adjustment. It is easy to achieve the perfect fit using elastic single pass, for example.

Finally, pass-through straps can be easily swapped out for different colors or materials, giving your G-Shock watch a whole new look.

Installing adapters on a G-Shock

Installing adapters on a G-Shock watch is a relatively straightforward process.

  1. First, you need to remove the existing strap from the lugs.
  2. Then, you attach the adapters to the lugs using the provided screws or pins.
  3. Finally, you slide the pass-through strap through the adapters, and you're good to go.

What is the fit like?

I find the fit to be better when compared to the stock resin straps. The stock straps flare out a bit, making a larger lug-to-lug span. With adapters, the G-Shock wears more comfortably as they follow the shape of my wrist and hug it better.

As far as the strap choices are concerned, I love how C&B Chevrons look with adapters. Also, any single-pass NATO looks and feels fantastic.

I tried thicker canvas pass-through straps, but they didn't feel as nice as they were chunkier and more robust.

Buying adapters

When buying, just make sure to check two dimensions:

  • The width of an attachment segment.
  • The width of the channel for the strap.

For example, I have adapters in 16mm/22mm size. These fit 5600 and 6900 series watches and can accommodate 22mm straps.

Many watch strap stores sell these adapters, but you can also check Amazon or eBay.

Adapters are a great addition to any G-Shock watch. They allow you to customize your watch to your liking while still maintaining the ruggedness and durability that G-Shock watches are known for.
