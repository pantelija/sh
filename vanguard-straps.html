About Vanguard Specialty Straps

Vanguard is a company that specializes in providing rubber watch straps, primarily designed for sports watches. The founder of Vanguard, who is a Tudor watch collector himself, wanted to create the perfect integrated rubber strap for his own Tudor watches, and the company was established.

Vanguard's rubber watch are waterproof and UV resistant, making them suitable for an active lifestyle. The straps are designed with an integrated ABS plastic insert, ensuring a seamless fit with the watch case.
