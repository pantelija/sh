What types of straps does Veblenist sell?

Here is a list of the different types of straps they offer:

Leather Nato Canvas

Width: Their bands are made in widths of 18mm, 19mm, 20mm, 21mm, and 22mm.

Two-piece straps are available in all listed sizes. Straps have a more pronounced taper at the buckle side. Tapering is either 3mm or 4mm.

Nylon natos are offered in 18mm, 20mm, and 22mm.

Material: Veblenist offers leather, nylon, and canvas straps. Their selection of leather is excellent. They have straps made from alligator leather, calfskin, cowhide, goatskin, nubuck, Shell Cordovan, and suede leather.

Canvas straps have leather keepers and they are lined with hypoallergenic and sweat-resistant French calfskin.

About Veblenist

Veblenist is a company dedicated to quality craftsmanship and unique design. They offer a range of strap types made from materials such as leather, alligator, calfskin and more. The company works with customers personally to create custom straps that reflect their unique essence.

Veblenist values their customers and considers them part of the family, offering a generous return policy and striving to fulfill all individual needs.
