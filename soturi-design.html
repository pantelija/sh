About Soturi Design

Soturi is a brand started by a Marine veteran and watch enthusiast with a passion for design and storytelling. The brand is inspired by warrior cultures and military designs throughout history, and focuses on creating high-quality, functional products with attention to detail.

The materials used are chosen for their durability and the designs aim to embody the timeless elements of simplicity, subtlety, and utility.

SOTURI is a Finnish word meaning warrior/soldier.
