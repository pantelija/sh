ColaReb® Launches ColaVeg: A Dedicated Website for Animal-Free and Vegan Watch Straps

Italian watch strap maker ColaReb® has always been about using high-quality and sustainable materials. Now they have gone a step further with ColaVeg, a whole new website fully dedicated to vegan leather watch straps. Their motto? "Italian Elegance without compromise. 100% animal-free watch straps."

Nenad Pantelic • July 27, 2024

ColaReb® is not new to eco-friendly products - they have been using it for years. But this new website, ColaVeg, is their special spot for watch straps that are totally cruelty and animal-free.

Straps are made from materials like apple skin, paper, wood, and super-fine microfiber. These materials look and feel like real leather, and they are extremely durable.

ColaVeg offers straps in a variety of sizes, including the popular 18mm, 20mm, and 22mm options. Some collections even include variants in less common widths like 19mm or 21mm.

The prices are fair. I believe the price range of $50 to $60 represents a solid value considering the quality of the product.

Congrats to ColaReb® For Leading the Way!

Hats off to ColaReb® for stepping up. Creating a new brand and offering their products through a new website is a bold move, and I'm rooting for them. Their initiative sets a positive example for others to follow. There are other brands offering vegan straps, but I don't believe any have dedicated their entire website to such products.

For more details, photos, and to purchase vegan straps, go directly to the collection page on the ColaVeg website.
