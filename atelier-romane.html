What types of straps does Atelier Romane sell?

Here is a list of the different types of straps they have available:

Leather Pass-through Rubber

Width: Their straps models are made in 16mm, 18mm, 19mm, 20mm, 21mm, or 22mm. Tapering is usually 2mm, but some sizes have a more pronounced taper (19mm/16mm, 20mm/16mm, 22mm/18mm etc).

About Atelier Romane

The company specializes in designing and producing watch straps. All their straps are 100% made in France. They prioritize using high-quality materials to ensure a luxurious product.

Atelier Romane carries a wide selection of straps, including rally straps, racing straps, and classic straps, in various lengths and widths. They even have watch straps for deployant buckles.
