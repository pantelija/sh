Ostrich leather watch straps

Ostrich straps have a unique texture and pattern, but they are also appreciated for their strength and suppleness.

Why we love them?

The pattern of bumps or empty quill follicles makes it easy to identify ostrich leather. This leather really has a sophisticated and elegant appearance. But what makes it stand out is its durability, toughness and softness.

  • One of the toughest leathers available
  • Leather contains natural oils that enhance durability
  • Straps have a unique pattern and texture

Average price of an ostrich watch strap

Ostrich straps are typically more expensive than other leather bands due to the rarity of the material and the time-consuming process of tanning and crafting the straps.

The cost is generally consistent across different stores, averaging between $120 and $160. Prices of made-to-order ostrich straps for luxury watches typically exceed $250.
