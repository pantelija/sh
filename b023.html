StrapHabit Owner Nick Lehner Shares Insights on Standard H Podcast

In a recent episode of the "Standard H" podcast, Nick Lehner, the owner of StrapHabit, engaged listeners for an hour in an insightful discussion covering a variety of topics. From heartfelt initiatives to childhood memories and entrepreneurial tips & tricks, Nick's appearance offered a multifaceted glimpse into his life and the journey of StrapHabit.

One of the highlights of the podcast was Nick's discussion about the collaboration between StrapHabit and Brodinkee to raise funds for cancer awareness and prevention - a cause close to his heart. This partnership exemplifies the company's commitment to making a positive impact beyond the realm of watch accessories.

Originating from upstate New York, Nick also shared personal stories about his upbringing, providing context to his journey as a business owner. He spoke about the genesis of StrapHabit in 2019 and the challenges and triumphs of starting and managing a business.

Running a Business

Nick also discussed the intricacies of managing stock and fulfillment across various platforms, including eBay, Amazon, and StrapHabit's own website. His expertise in navigating the e-commerce gave us a glimpse into the behind-the-scenes operations of a growing business.

Beyond business matters, the conversation sometimes went off course into Nick's passion for cars and his observations on the world of watches and watch straps.

In addition to Nick's insights, credit must be given to Wesley Smith, the podcast host, whose engaging demeanor and questioning style contributed significantly to the episode's success. Smith's ability to pose thought-provoking and innovative questions ensured a seamless flow of conversation.

Wesley's facilitation created an environment where Nick could comfortably share his experiences and perspectives.
