About Rubber B Watch Bands & Straps

Rubber B is a luxury company based in Switzerland that creates innovative rubber watch straps, which are the first of their kind. The straps feature the legal "Swiss-made" marking and are made by top leaders in the luxury watch industry.

Rubber B was the first company to produce curved-end integrated rubber straps for Rolex watches in 2010. They launched their first models for sale in April 2011, which were designed for the Daytona and Submariner models and allowed the owner to mount their own Rolex deployant oyster clasp.

The company has multiple trademarks. For example, SwimSkinÂ® is a trademark of Rubber B, LLC that creates a rubber surface that mimics the look and feel of various organic materials, including canvas, alligator hide, and more.

Rubber B has had several collaborations with luxury watch brands, including Manufacture Roger Dubuis and Rolex. They have also partnered with independent Swiss companies, such as i-Unique, for the production and distribution of universal watch bands. Many independent brands and celebrities have chosen Rubber B straps for their watches, including Bamford Watch Department and Kevin O'Leary from "Shark Tank".

The company's reputation is based on the quality of its products and customer service. Driven by a passion for luxury innovation, Rubber B has designed straps that harmonize with the aesthetic of the intended watch without compromising its beauty, making it a good option for athletes, divers, outdoor enthusiasts, and luxury watch owners looking for a comfortable alternative to metal bracelets, leather bands, and NATO straps.
