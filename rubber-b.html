About Rubber B Watch Bands & Straps

Rubber B is a luxury company based in Switzerland that creates innovative rubber watch straps, which are the first of their kind. The straps feature the legal "Swiss-made" marking and are made by top leaders in the luxury watch industry.

The company's reputation is based on the quality of its products and customer service. Driven by a passion for luxury innovation, Rubber B has designed straps that harmonize with the aesthetic of the intended watch without compromising its beauty, making it a good option for athletes, divers, outdoor enthusiasts, and luxury watch owners looking for a comfortable alternative to metal bracelets, leather bands, and NATO straps.
