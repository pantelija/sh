What types of straps does Everest Bands sell?

Everest Bands manufactures and sells rubber and leather straps.

Leather Rubber Custom

Width: They offer curved-end rubber or leather straps that fit the most popular watches from Rolex, Tudor, and Panerai. In addition, they sell universal rubber straps for watches with the 20mm lug spacing. Universal straps have a straight-end.

Material: For their rubber straps they use Swiss-made vulcanized rubber. Vulcanization improves the properties of the rubber by increasing its strength, flexibility, and resistance to heat and other environmental factors.

Everest products are resistant to UV, dust, water, stain, heat, cold, stretch, tear, and various chemicals.

What makes them special?

Everest took strap engineering to the next level. To get there they introduced multiple patents. Everest straps use a unique steel end-link system, as well as other advanced technologies. The result is a strap that perfectly conforms to the shape of the case and can be used with a Rolex clasp.

Each component of an Everest strap is produced to the tolerance level only attainable by the most elite Swiss watch companies.

About Everest Bands

Everest Horology Products is a company that offers aftermarket watch straps and accessories for Rolex, Tudor, and Panerai watches. The company was started by CEO and head of product development, Michael, with the help of his friend and partner David. They have spent years developing high-quality designs and materials to create the best aftermarket watch straps available for these brands.

The company began with a goal to innovate products and offer only the highest quality materials. Michael thought up the idea for the company after the birth of his first son, recognizing the lack of custom strap solutions for Rolex watch owners.
