About Holben's

Mike, the owner of Holben's Fine Watches and Bands, began his love of wristwatches as a child with a 1976 Tony the Tiger watch. He went on to acquire a variety of styles, developing restoration skills and learning about watch history and technology while studying architectural preservation. He started selling items from his collection on eBay, which eventually turned into a full-time business.

The company, founded in 1998, also includes Holben's Fine Watch Bands, a store for wristwatch-related accessories. The company is based on an island in the Pacific Northwest and prides itself on personal service and quality craftsmanship, focusing on European and American manufacturers.
