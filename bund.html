Bund watch straps

A two-piece strap with an extra layer of material placed underneath the watch, creating a cuff-style band.

Click to see the stores

Why we love them?

If you can pull it off they look pretty cool. Especially on military watches. The name "Bund" comes from the German word "Bundeswehr," which means "armed forces," as these straps were originally developed for use by military personnel.

  • Better weight distribution across the wrist
  • They are very comfortable
  • The additional layer prevents skin irritation

What it the average price of a bund strap?

The cost of bund watch straps can vary depending on the brand, design, and level of quality. Prices can range from as low as $30 or $40 to as high as $250 or more.

The average cost of a bund strap is around $100.
