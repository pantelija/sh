About Condor Straps

Condor Straps has been in operation since 1939 and specializes in the production of watch straps. They are based in the UK but have a wide international distribution network. They emphasize craftsmanship and offer high-quality products at fair prices.

The company values traditional teachings and years of experience, which have enabled them to create a diverse collection of well-made watch straps. They carefully select materials such as leather, nylon, silicone, and metal to ensure durability and undergo rigorous quality tests, including strength, humidity, and wear tests.

Condor's range of watch straps comes in various colors and textures, including reds, blues, smooth, and patent finishes. These options allow customers to easily find the perfect match to add a final touch to their watches.

The company's designs are carefully thought out and incorporate considered details. They offer a timeless choice for customers who want to refresh their watches or adapt them to current trends.
