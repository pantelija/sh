Rubber watch straps

Rubber watch straps are known for their durability and resistance to water, making them a popular choice for sports watches and other watches that may be exposed to water or other elements.

Why we love them?

Rubber watch straps are more comfortable to wear than other types of two-piece watch straps, as the rubber material is flexible and can mold to the shape of the wrist.

  • Good ones are very comfortable
  • Water and UV resistant
  • Durable and long-lasting
  • They are easy to clean and maintain
  • Available in a wide range of colors and styles

Average price of a rubber strap

The price of rubber watch straps can vary depending on the brand, style, and quality. Some rubber watch straps can be purchased for as little as $20 or $30, while others can cost several hundred dollars.

In general, lower-priced rubber watch straps are made from synthetic rubber and may not be as long-lasting as higher-priced straps. Higher-priced rubber straps are often made from natural rubber and are designed to be more durable and of better quality.

Common types of rubber straps

There are several types of rubber watch straps that are commonly used. These include:

  • Perforated & vented rubber straps
  • Rubber NATO (pass-through) straps
  • Rubber + leather hybrid straps
  • Silicone watch straps
  • FKM rubber watch straps
  • Natural rubber watch straps
  • Tropical style straps
  • Waffle pattern straps
