About Milano Straps

Milano Straps is a family-owned business founded by Antonio and his wife Maria. Antonio is a USA-based Italian watchmaker with a passion for watches that dates back to his childhood. He grew up surrounded by watches, as his father was also a watchmaker. In 1984, Antonio opened a vintage shop in Milan, starting his first watch business.

The idea for Milano Straps was born out of Antonio's realization that watch straps lacked variety in colors and styles. He wanted to break the rules and offer unique and high-quality watch straps that would enhance the watch-wearing experience.

Milano Straps combines the rich Italian culture and history with modern designs and fresh ideas. The company's mission is to make watch straps more than just an accessory, but a statement piece and a reflection of the wearer's personality. The company prides itself on its dedication to Italian tradition and craftsmanship. Milano Straps collaborates with some of the best Italian artisans and uses top-quality Italian leather in the creation of their straps. The leather is tanned without the use of harmful chemicals, ensuring a high level of quality and attention to detail.
