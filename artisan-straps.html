What types of straps does Artisan Straps sell?

Artisan Straps exclusively offers two-piece leather straps.

Leather Custom

Width: They offer straps for watches with 17mm, 18mm, 19mm, 20mm, 21mm, or 22mm lug widths. And the best part: most of the collections have straps in every single one of the listed sizes.

Material: Artisan Straps uses leather for their straps. The leather selection is very nice: alligator, goatskin, Epsom, Pueblo, Shell Cordovan, Cromexcel, suede, to list the most common options.

About Artisan Straps

Artisan Straps creates handmade, high-quality watch straps. The company's founder, Jayson, was inspired by his grandfather's passion for creating leather straps for his Rolex, which was passed down to him.

Jayson's search for the perfect strap led him to create Artisan Straps to provide quality straps at a reasonable price to watch lovers. The straps are made by a small team of talented individuals in their Sydney workshop. They offer assistance in choosing the right strap for a watch through their email and other channels of communication.
