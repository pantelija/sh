About Horus Straps

Horus Watch Straps was established in 2014 and has since become a prominent player in the watch strap industry. They have gained recognition for their impeccable ergonomic design, which sets their watch straps apart in terms of comfort and aesthetics.

The company specializes in creating watch straps that perfectly complement Swiss manufactured watches from renowned brands such as Audemars Piguet, Panerai, Rolex, Breitling, Tag Heuer, Bell & Ross, and more. Horus Straps has established a network of authorized retailers in over fifty cities across five continents, making their products easily accessible to customers worldwide.

They prioritize the customer experience and actively engage with their audience, considering it of utmost importance. The company's mission is to inspire individuals to challenge the norm, surpass boundaries, and stand out from the crowd.
