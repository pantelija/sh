What types of straps does Clockwork Synergy sell?

Here is the list of strap types they offer:

Width: They offer straps for watches with any lug spacing between 18mm and 16mm. Their most popular models are made in 18mm, 20mm, or 22mm widths.

It is praiseworthy that many of their collections have models in odd sizes (i.e. 19mm, 21mm).

Material: Clockwork Synergy sources straps and replacement bands made from leather, sailcloth, canvas, rubber, silicone, and nylon.

Materials come in various forms. For example they offer standard, elastic, seat-belt, heavy-duty, or braided nylon straps.

About Clockwork Synergy

Clockwork Synergy is a provider of quality watch straps and bands for over 10 years. It started as a small e-commerce business on eBay in 2005, with a focus on easy-to-swap watch bands.

The company has since expanded and now sells on multiple sites internationally, has a diverse product line, and has grown to hire a team of six.

The company values supporting small businesses and recently launched a campaign to support the restoration projects of the city of Ellicott City after a devastating flood. Their office is located in a beautifully restored 200-year-old building in Downtown Ellicott City.
